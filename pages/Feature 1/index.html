Most Played Champs

My crush most played champion that she truly enjoys and has fun always when she uses these champs

Lux

Luxanna Crownguard, commonly known as Lux, is a powerful mage from Demacia. She wields light magic, capable of manipulating it for both offensive and defensive purposes. Lux is known for her versatility, often playing roles as a support or mid-lane mage.

Seraphine

Seraphine is a contemporary popstar from Piltover, introduced as a champion with a strong connection to music and magical abilities. She uses her voice and musical talents to support her allies and disrupt enemies on the battlefield.

Caitlyn

Caitlyn, known as the Sheriff of Piltover, is a renowned marksman and law enforcer from the technologically advanced city of Piltover. Armed with her trusty rifle, Caitlyn excels at long-range combat, utilizing her precision and various traps to control the battlefield and take down enemies from a distance. She is often played as an AD carry, leading her team to victory with her sharpshooting skills and strategic prowess.

Ahri

A nine-tailed fox spirit from Ionia, who possesses the ability to manipulate and absorb the life essence of her enemies. She is an elusive mage-assassin, known for her charm and seductive nature, often luring unsuspecting prey to their demise.
